Sully Dog

On Thursday 27th June 2019, our beloved Sully Dog went to sleep for the last time. He was only six years old and developed an auto-immune illness called Thrombocytopenia, common in middle-aged spaniels. Though devastated by his loss, we want to remember the good times, and this site is a tribute to him. Love you buddy, won't be long.

When we got Sully in 2013, I (having never had a dog) had absolutely no idea what I was getting myself in for. My wife tried to tell me, but I wouldn't listen. It'll be fine, I said, it'll be easy, I said.
